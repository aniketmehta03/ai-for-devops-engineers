# Day 10 Observations

## Personal Observations

CI/CD pipelines generate large amounts of information.

AI helps summarize logs and quickly identify potential failure points.

---

## What Worked Well

* AI explained pipeline stages clearly.
* AI generated useful RCA drafts.
* Structured prompts improved analysis quality.
* AI reduced time spent reading logs.

---

## Limitations

AI may:

* Misinterpret incomplete logs
* Assume incorrect configurations
* Miss organization-specific workflows

Therefore, manual validation is essential.

---

## Engineering Mindset

Pipelines produce evidence.

AI analyzes evidence.

Engineers make deployment decisions.

---

## Best Practices

Always:

* Verify logs
* Review workflow configuration
* Check secrets and environment variables
* Validate infrastructure
* Test fixes before deployment

---

## Daily Reflection

Today's learning reinforced that AI should assist engineers in understanding failures rather than making autonomous deployment decisions.

Rule:

AI accelerates investigation.

Engineers approve changes.
