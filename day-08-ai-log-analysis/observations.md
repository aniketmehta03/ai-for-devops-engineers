# Day 08 Observations

## Personal Observations

AI is highly effective at organizing and summarizing large amounts of log data.

Instead of reading thousands of lines manually, AI can quickly identify recurring patterns and probable causes.

---

## What Worked Well

* Structured prompts improved analysis quality.
* AI generated concise executive summaries.
* Root Cause Analysis became easier to understand.
* Incident reports were organized and readable.

---

## Limitations

AI may:

* Miss environment-specific context
* Misinterpret incomplete logs
* Make assumptions without evidence

Therefore, verification is mandatory.

---

## Engineering Mindset

Logs provide evidence.

AI provides analysis.

Engineers make decisions.

---

## Best Practices

Always:

* Collect sufficient logs
* Provide context
* Request assumptions
* Verify recommendations
* Validate fixes before deployment

---

## Daily Reflection

Today's learning showed that AI is a powerful assistant for incident investigation but should never replace evidence-based engineering.

Rule:

AI accelerates analysis.

Engineers validate conclusions.
