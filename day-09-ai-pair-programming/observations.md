# Day 09 Observations

## Personal Observations

AI is most valuable when used as a collaborator rather than a replacement for engineering knowledge.

---

## What Worked Well

* AI generated useful boilerplate code.
* AI explained infrastructure concepts clearly.
* AI improved documentation quality.
* AI accelerated repetitive tasks.

---

## Limitations

AI may:

* Produce insecure code
* Make incorrect assumptions
* Ignore organization-specific requirements
* Suggest outdated practices

Manual review is always required.

---

## Engineering Mindset

AI writes drafts.

Engineers build systems.

AI suggests.

Engineers decide.

---

## Best Practices

Always:

* Review generated code
* Test automation
* Validate infrastructure
* Check security
* Verify before deployment

---

## Daily Reflection

Today's learning changed my perspective on AI.

The goal is not to become dependent on AI.

The goal is to become a more productive engineer by combining engineering knowledge with AI assistance.

Rule:

AI accelerates development.

Engineers own production.
