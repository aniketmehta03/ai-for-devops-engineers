# Day 06 Observations

## Observations

* AI performs significantly better when given clear context.
* Structured prompts generate more consistent and useful responses.
* Role-based prompting improves technical accuracy.
* Asking for verification steps reduces the risk of incorrect implementation.
* Requesting assumptions and risks makes AI responses more production-oriented.

---

## Personal Learnings

Instead of asking:

> Fix my deployment.

I should ask:

* What is the root cause?
* What commands should I run to verify it?
* What is the safest production fix?
* What rollback strategy should I follow?
* What monitoring should be added?

---

## Engineering Mindset

Good engineers don't ask better AI.

They ask better questions.

AI should be used to:

* Accelerate troubleshooting
* Improve documentation
* Review infrastructure
* Analyze incidents

AI should not replace engineering judgment.

---

## Rule Going Forward

AI assists.

Engineers verify.

Production safety comes first.

---

## Daily Reflection

Today's learning shifted my mindset from using AI as a chatbot to using AI as an engineering assistant.

My goal is to build a reusable prompt library that improves productivity across Kubernetes, Terraform, AWS, CI/CD, and Platform Engineering workflows.
